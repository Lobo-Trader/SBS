# Pattern Definition

## Market Structure Break (MSB)
The Market Structure Break (MSB) is a concept used in technical analysis to identify points where the price structure changes direction, signaling a potential trend reversal. Understanding MSB is crucial for traders as it often indicates new opportunities in the market.
